## Executive Summary
The Ethiopia Cosmos Learning Community is an innovative, student-led initiative designed to bridge the gap in astronomy and space science education for Ethiopian youth aged 13–20. Recognizing the critical lack of structured, engaging scientific resources in our region, our student group has successfully developed and deployed a fully functional Progressive Web Application (PWA).

Built using a modern technical stack including GitHub, Supabase, Vercel, LiveKit, and Groq AI, the platform seamlessly integrates structured academic lessons, downloadable materials, progress tracking, and interactive testing. To foster deep engagement, it also features an intelligent AI learning assistant, a real-time community chat, a live hosting system for virtual meetings, and an educational, short-form video scrolling feed.

## The Vision
Look up on a clear night, and you will see countless stars — yet for so many teenagers, the wonders of the universe remain frustratingly out of reach. Astronomy is barely covered in our educational curriculum, and existing online resources are often too advanced, scattered, or not a match for our Ethiopian teenagers. Ethio Cosmos was built to provide a single digital space for every curious teen in Ethiopia to learn, test themselves, and genuinely grow as young astronomers.

## Mission, Vision & Target Audience
*   **Mission:** We are committed to providing a comprehensive, multilingual, and highly interactive learning platform focused on astronomy. By leveraging community-driven content, collaborative live sessions, and AI support, we strive to make complex scientific concepts both engaging and accessible to all native language speakers in Ethiopia.
*   **Vision:** To cultivate a future where every teenager in Ethiopia has access to world-class scientific knowledge, empowering them to explore the cosmos and drive innovation within their communities.
*   **Target Audience:** Teenagers and young adults in Ethiopia (13 to 20 years old) eager to expand their understanding of the universe and connect with fellow science enthusiasts.

## What Was Built
*   **Structured Curriculum:** Dedicated astronomy lessons and organized topics with downloadable study materials.
*   **Progress Tracking & Assessment:** Interactive tests paired with a tracking system for personal academic growth.
*   **Smart AI Assistant:** An embedded AI guide powered by Groq to answer student questions instantly, acting as an on-demand tutor.
*   **Social Learning Feed:** A TikTok-style scrolling feed strictly dedicated to short-form educational videos uploaded by community members.
*   **Live Collaboration Tools:** High-fidelity live community chats and a virtual "Live Host" meeting session system for real-time peer teaching and discussions.

## Technology Stack
*   **Next.js:** React framework powering the fast, responsive user interface.
*   **Supabase:** PostgreSQL database with row-level security and auth integration.
*   **Google OAuth:** Secure student login using existing Google accounts.
*   **Vercel:** Cloud hosting with automatic deployment.
*   **GitHub:** Version control tracking all code, issues, and milestones.
*   **Groq:** AI source to answer user questions.

## Future Roadmap
*   **Multilingual Expansion:** Integration of Amharic and Afan Oromo alongside English.
*   **Broadening Educational Scope:** Expanding to general science hub covering multiple disciplines.
*   **Native Android Application:** Priority development of a dedicated Android app for offline access.
